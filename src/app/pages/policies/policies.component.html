Cottage Garden Menus

Here are our Menus, with some seasonal tweaks, our soup is made with organic fresh vegetables (sometimes frozen but still organic), sweet potato, few white potato, butternut squash, zucchini, summer squash, kale or spinach, red lentils, peas, corn, onions, celery and carrots.
Snacks are always fresh fruit or veggies and bread or crackers.
In the very warm weather I make popsicles from real fruit juice and water for an occasional snack.
We also may have a picnic lunch with sandwiches; tuna, or grilled cheese, carrot sticks, guacamole or hummus and fresh fruit.
Breakfast cereals are whole grain and we grind the rice to make the cereal. No dairy is used, just water and a bit of maple syrup.

Snow Day Policy:

PLEASE DO NOT ASSUME WE ARE CLOSED!!!
We will make every effort to be open on snow days.
However, depending on the severity of the weather and our ability to ensure
safety for you and your children, we reserve the right to close for either the whole, or part of the day.
We will make our decision by 6:30am and let each of you know by text, email or phone no later than 7:00am.
PLEASE CHECK YOUR MESSAGES!!
