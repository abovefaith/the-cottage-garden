Who We Are

The Cottage Garden is a unique home-based Waldorf Day Care program, licensed for up to ten children.
We offer a safe, loving environment filled with simple handmade toys and developmentally appropriate activities, all created to enhance the young child’s innate sense of learning through imitation. Our day is a balance of structure and free play. The rhythm of our daily activities offers a warm, and well-guided first playgroup experience for your pre-school child—a wonderful transition between home and school.
